# History

The **History** tab shows all past phases for the game. Click any phase to view its content.

## Navigating History

Phases are listed in order. Click a phase to open it. Use the **Back** button to return to the phase list.

## Common Room Phases in History

Past Common Room phases show the same Posts, New Comments, and Polls tabs as the active Common Room. Content is read-only — no new posts or replies can be made.

## Action Phases in History

Past action phases have two sub-tabs:

- **Submissions** — Your submitted action for that phase
- **Results** — The GM's result for your action

Players see only their own submission and result. Action text and results are collapsible — click to expand long entries.

---

## GM: History View

GMs see all players' submissions and results, not just their own. The History tab shows the same data available in the All Submissions view during an active phase, preserved after the phase ends.
