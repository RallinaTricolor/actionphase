# Getting Started

This page walks through the first things you'll do when you join ActionPhase — finding a game, applying, and getting oriented once you're in.

## Your Dashboard

After logging in you land on the **Dashboard**, which shows all your active games at a glance.

### Game Cards

Each game you're in appears as a card showing:

- **Your role** — Player, GM, or Audience
- **Game state** — Recruiting, In Progress, Paused, etc.
- **Current phase** — The active phase name and type, with its deadline if one is set
- **Indicators** — Unread message count, pending applications (GMs), and whether you have an action to submit

Cards with an **Urgent** badge have an approaching deadline with an action still needed from you. An **Urgent Actions Required** section appears at the top of the dashboard when any of your player games needs immediate attention, with a link to each urgent game.

### Upcoming Deadlines

On desktop, a sidebar shows upcoming deadlines across your games, color-coded by urgency: red for under 6 hours, yellow for 6–24 hours, blue for further out.

### Recent Activity

A **Recent Activity** panel shows the latest posts, comments, and private messages across your games, each linking directly to that content.

## Finding a Game

Go to the **Games** page from the navigation. Games are listed with their title, description, genre, current state, and player count.

**To narrow the list:**

- **Search** — Type in the search bar to filter by title or description
- **State** — Filter by game state (Recruiting, In Progress, etc.)
- **All Games / My Games / Applied / Not Joined** — Quick filters for your participation status
- **Has Open Spots** — Show only games that still have room for players
- **Sort** — Order by Recent Activity, Recently Created, Starting Soon, or Alphabetical

## Applying to a Game

When you find a game in **Recruiting** state, click **Apply to Join**. The form has two fields:

- **Role** — Choose **Player** to actively participate or **Audience** to follow along without playing
- **Application Message** — Optional note to the GM introducing yourself or your character ideas

Submit and your application shows as **Pending** until the GM reviews it.

Once a game has moved past Recruitment, you can still join as an **audience member** by clicking **Join as Audience**. See [Audience](./audience) for what audience members can see.

## Once You're In

After your application is approved:

1. Wait for the GM to move the game to **Character Creation**
2. Go to the **People** tab and click **Create Character**
3. Fill out your character and submit — the GM will review and approve it
4. Once approved, you're ready to play when the GM starts the game

For the full character creation flow, see [Characters](./characters).

## Navigating a Game

The game page uses tabs to organize content. Which tabs appear depends on the game's current state and active phase type — not all tabs are visible at all times.

During an active game (**In Progress**) the main tabs are:

- **Common Room** — Posts, discussion, and polls (only during Common Room phases)
- **Submit Action** / **Actions** — Submit your action or view all submissions as GM (only during Action phases)
- **Phases** — Phase management panel (GM only)
- **People** — Characters and participants
- **Messages** — Private conversations between characters (visible to players, GM, and audience members with assigned NPCs)
- **Handouts** — Reference materials shared by the GM
- **Audience** — Read-only view of all submissions and private messages (GM and audience members only)
- **History** — All past phases

During **Recruitment**, you see Handouts and Game Info (GMs also see an Applications tab). During **Character Creation**, you see People and Handouts. After the game ends, you see History, People, Handouts, and Game Info.

Deadlines are not a separate tab — they appear in the strip at the top of the game page.

## Notifications

The bell icon in the navigation bar shows unread notifications. Click it for a dropdown, or go to the full **Notifications** page to see everything. Notifications are created for:

- New private messages
- Replies to your comments
- Character mentions (when your character is @mentioned in a comment)
- Action results published by the GM
- New phases and polls
- Application and character approval decisions
- Game state changes

Click any notification to go directly to the relevant content. Unread notifications are highlighted; read ones are dimmed.
