# Creating an Account

Get started with ActionPhase by creating your free account.

## Step 1: Navigate to ActionPhase

Visit [https://action-phase.com](https://action-phase.com) in your web browser.

## Step 2: Sign Up

1. Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Email**: Your email address (for verification and notifications)
   - **Username**: Your display name (visible to other players)
   - **Password**: Choose a secure password

## Step 3: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email for a verification message from ActionPhase
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. You'll be redirected back to ActionPhase

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

Once verified, you can:
- Upload an avatar (optional)
- Update your profile settings
- Set your notification preferences

## Next Steps

Now that you have an account:

1. **[Join a Game](/user/getting-started/joining-game)** - Find your first game

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive verification email?**
- Check your spam folder
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support if the issue persists

**Forgot your password?**
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter your email to receive a reset link
