# Understanding Game Phases

ActionPhase games operate in a unique phase-based system that structures gameplay into distinct periods of activity. This guide explains how phases work and what you can do in each one.

## Understanding Game States vs. Phases

**Important Distinction:**

**Game States** describe where the game is in its overall lifecycle:
- Setup, Recruitment, Character Creation, In Progress, Paused, Completed, Cancelled

**Phases** are the gameplay cycles that happen during the "In Progress" state:
- Common Room Phase and Action Phase alternate during active gameplay

Think of it this way: The **game state** is like the season you're in, while **phases** are the individual days within that season.

## The Phase Cycle

Once a game reaches "In Progress" state, it operates in a repeating cycle of two phase types:

```
Common Room Phase → Action Phase → (Results Published) → Common Room Phase → ...
```

This cycle repeats throughout the game until the GM ends it.

## Phase Types

### Common Room Phase
**When:** Between action rounds
**Duration:** Set by your GM (often 24-72 hours)
**What you can do:**
- Post in-character messages in the Common Room
- React to other players' posts
- Have conversations and roleplay
- Build character relationships
- Plan strategies with other players
- Share information you've learned

The Common Room is the social hub of your game. Think of it as the tavern, spaceship lounge, or base camp where characters interact between adventures. This is where most roleplay happens.

**Tips for Common Room:**
- Stay in character when posting
- Use the @ mention feature to address specific characters
- React to posts to show your character's emotions
- Check back regularly - conversations move quickly!

### Action Phase
**When:** Time for your character to take action
**Duration:** Set by your GM (typically 2-5 days)
**What you can do:**
- Submit what your character does
- Edit your action submission before the deadline
- View the action prompt from your GM
- Continue posting in the Common Room (but most players focus on writing actions)

The Action Phase is when you decide what your character actually does. Your GM will provide a prompt or situation, and you respond with your character's actions.

**How to Submit Actions:**
1. Click "Submit Action" on the game page
2. Write what your character does (be specific!)
3. Save your submission (you can edit it anytime before the deadline)
4. Finalize before the deadline
5. Wait for GM to publish results

**Action Tips:**
- Be clear and specific about what you're trying to do
- Include your character's thoughts and motivations
- Reference items or abilities you want to use
- Mention contingency plans ("If X doesn't work, I'll try Y")
- Submit early so you can revise later if needed

### Results

**Results are not a separate phase type** - they're published by the GM during an Action Phase.

**What happens:**
- After the Action Phase deadline passes, the GM reads all actions
- The GM writes individualized results for each player
- When ready, the GM publishes the results
- Each player receives their private results
- The GM then creates a new Common Room phase to continue the game

**Reading Your Results:**
- You'll get a notification when results are published
- Your results are private - only you and the GM see them
- Results tell you what happened when your character tried their action
- They include new information, consequences, and complications
- Other players don't see your results unless you share them

## Game States (Lifecycle)

Before reaching the repeating phase cycle, games go through these states:

### Setup
**When:** GM is first creating the game
**What's happening:** GM configures game settings, writes description, sets parameters
**What you can do:** Nothing yet - the game isn't visible to players

### Recruitment
**When:** GM is accepting new players
**What you can do:**
- Browse and read the game description
- Apply to join the game
- See how many spots are available
- See other players who have applied

The GM reviews applications and approves players. Once approved, you move to character creation.

### Character Creation
**When:** After recruitment closes, before gameplay starts
**What you can do:**
- Create your character
- Submit character for GM approval
- Revise character based on GM feedback
- Wait for all players to have approved characters

Once all characters are approved, the GM starts the game.

### In Progress
**When:** Active gameplay
**What happens:** The repeating phase cycle (Common Room → Action) runs continuously
**Duration:** Weeks or months, depending on the game

This is the main gameplay state where the phase cycle happens.

### Paused
**When:** GM temporarily suspends the game
**What you can do:**
- Read past phases and posts
- Cannot submit new actions or posts
- Wait for GM to resume

Games can be paused for GM vacation, holidays, or other reasons.

### Completed
**When:** The game has ended normally
**What happens:**
- Final phase concludes the story
- Game becomes read-only
- Some action submissions and results may become public (GM's choice)
- Game archive is preserved

### Cancelled
**When:** The game ended early (didn't complete normally)
**What happens:** Similar to Completed, but indicates the game didn't finish its story

## Phase Advancement

Your GM controls when phases advance. They might:
- Set automatic deadlines (e.g., "Action Phase ends in 48 hours")
- Advance manually when everyone is ready
- Wait for all players to submit before advancing
- Use a combination of these methods

**Deadline Notifications:**
- You'll see countdown timers when deadlines are set
- Email notifications remind you of upcoming deadlines (if enabled)
- The game page always shows the current phase and time remaining

## Common Room Availability

**Important:** The Common Room is always available, even during Action Phases!

You can post in the Common Room during both phase types:
- **During Common Room Phase**: Primary discussion time, most active
- **During Action Phase**: Still available for quick coordination, though most players focus on writing their actions

The Common Room never closes - it just becomes less active during Action Phases as players work on their submissions.

## Making the Most of Each Phase

### Common Room Strategy
- Coordinate with other players about plans
- Share information your character learned
- Build alliances and relationships
- Set up strategies for the next action phase
- Ask questions about the game world
- React to what happened in the last action results

### Action Phase Strategy
- Read your GM's prompt carefully
- Consider what your character would realistically do
- Think about consequences before submitting
- Coordinate timing with other players if needed (via Common Room or private messages)
- Don't wait until the last minute to submit
- Leave time to revise your action if you think of improvements

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I miss an Action Phase deadline?**
A: Your character takes no action that round. The GM might describe your character as hesitating, being indecisive, or missing the opportunity.

**Q: Can I change my action after submitting?**
A: Yes! You can edit your action as many times as you want before the deadline. Once the deadline passes, actions lock and cannot be changed.

**Q: How long do phases typically last?**
A: It varies by game and GM preference:
- Common Room: 1-3 days typically
- Action Phase: 2-5 days typically
- Results are usually published within 1-3 days after the Action deadline

**Q: Can I post in Common Room during Action Phase?**
A: Yes! The Common Room is available during both phase types. However, most players focus on writing their action submissions during Action Phases, so the Common Room is usually quieter.

**Q: What happens if everyone submits early?**
A: Many GMs advance the phase early if all players have submitted, but some wait for the deadline regardless. Ask your GM about their preference.

**Q: Can I see other players' action submissions?**
A: No. Action submissions are completely private between each player and the GM. You only see what others tell you in the Common Room or private messages.

## Tips for New Players

1. **Understand the Rhythm:** Games have a natural rhythm - Common Room for discussion, Action for doing things, results for consequences, repeat
2. **Read Everything:** Read all Common Room posts and results to understand the story
3. **Use Common Room:** Ask other characters (in character) about the world during Common Room phases
4. **Take Notes:** Keep track of important information between phases
5. **Be Patient:** Phases move at their own pace - check in regularly but don't expect instant responses
6. **Communicate:** Let your GM know if you'll be away and might miss a deadline
7. **Have Fun:** The phase system creates natural story beats - embrace the rhythm!
